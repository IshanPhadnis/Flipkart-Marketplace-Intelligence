## 3. Month-over-Month (MoM) Revenue Growth Analysis

| **Highest Positive Growth** | **5442.29% (Jun 2024)** |

| **Strongest Revenue Month** | **Nov 2024 (₹894,366)** |
| **Latest MoM Growth** | **53.15% (Sep 2025)** |
| **Consecutive Positive Growth (2025)** | **Feb–Apr 2025 (3 Months)** |

---

### 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue experienced significant month-to-month fluctuations throughout the analysis period, indicating varying customer demand and promotional activity.
- **June 2024** recorded the highest month-over-month growth (**5442.29%**) due to a sharp recovery from an unusually weak **May 2024**.
- The largest decline occurred in **April 2023 (-97.57%)**, immediately following a high-performing March, suggesting the impact of seasonality or campaign completion.
- Revenue grew consistently for **three consecutive months (Feb–Apr 2025)** before declining in May, indicating a temporary upward trend.
- The business finished the dataset strongly, with revenue increasing by **53.15% in September 2025**, suggesting positive sales momentum.

---

### 💡 Business Recommendations

- Investigate the business events, campaigns, or product launches that drove the exceptional growth in **June 2024** and **November 2024**.
- Analyze months with significant declines to determine whether inventory shortages, seasonality, or reduced marketing activity contributed to lower sales.

---

### 🔍 Key Insights

- **UPI** was the most preferred payment method, generating **₹2,025,849**, accounting for **32.88%** of total revenue.
- **Wallets** ranked second with **₹1,610,005 (26.13%)**, demonstrating strong adoption of digital payment methods.

- Strengthen partnerships with UPI providers by offering cashback and promotional campaigns to encourage continued usage.
- Continue investing in digital payment experiences, as nearly **59%** of revenue comes from UPI and Wallet transactions.
- Investigate why Card payments lag behind other digital methods and identify opportunities to improve customer adoption.
- Monitor payment trends regularly to optimize promotional offers and payment gateway strategies.

- Develop targeted campaigns to convert Medium Value customers into High Value customers by increasing purchase frequency and basket size.
- Analyze purchasing behavior of High Value customers to identify patterns that can be replicated across other customer segments.
- Consider cost-effective marketing strategies for Low Value customers, as their revenue contribution is currently minimal.
